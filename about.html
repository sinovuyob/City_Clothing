Our Story

From urban streets to global fashion - discover the journey of City Clothing

Who We Are

City Clothing was born from a passion for urban culture and a vision to create clothing that speaks to the modern individual. Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small local boutique to a recognized name in streetwear fashion.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to express their unique style through high-quality, affordable urban fashion that combines comfort, durability, and contemporary design.

Our Vision

To become the leading global brand for urban fashion, inspiring confidence and self-expression through innovative designs and sustainable practices.

Our Values

Quality craftsmanship, customer satisfaction, innovation, sustainability, and community engagement are at the core of everything we do.

Our Journey

2015

Founded with a single store in downtown, focusing on local urban fashion trends.

2018

Expanded to 5 locations nationwide and launched our e-commerce platform.

2021

Went international with shipping to over 50 countries and 100,000+ customers.

2024

Recognized as "Urban Fashion Brand of the Year" at the Fashion Excellence Awards.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We believe in fashion that doesn't cost the earth. That's why we're committed to sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

Eco-Friendly Materials

Over 60% of our collection uses sustainable and recycled materials.

Ethical Manufacturing

All our partners follow fair labor practices and maintain safe working conditions.

Carbon Neutral Shipping

We offset 100% of our shipping emissions through verified carbon credits.
