Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags. Returns are free for store credit, or you can exchange for a different size/color.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping times vary by location.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website or the carrier's site.
